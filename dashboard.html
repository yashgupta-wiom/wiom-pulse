Release Knowledge Base
Recharge Flow v1 — Nov 2025▼
What shipped: First UPI-based recharge with saved payment methods.
Expected: +15% success rate
Actual: +8% success rate (missed target)
Learnings: Feature phone users struggled with UPI deep-link. Need fallback for non-UPI devices.
Follow-up: Added SMS-based recharge fallback (shipped Dec '25).
OTP Revamp — Mar 2026▼
What shipped: WhatsApp OTP + auto-read fallback for SMS OTP.
Expected: +5% login success
Actual: +9.4% login success (exceeded target)
Learnings: WhatsApp OTP delivery is 3x faster than SMS in Tier-2/3. Auto-read reduced friction significantly.
Follow-up: Evaluate extending WhatsApp OTP to recharge flow.
Plan Comparison Page — Feb 2026▼
What shipped: Side-by-side plan comparison with savings calculator.
Expected: +8% plan upgrades
Actual: +14% plan upgrades (exceeded target by 75%)
Learnings: Savings calculator was the top-engaged element. Users spend 2.3x more time on page when calculator is used.
Follow-up: Expand to Tier-2 cities. Add annual vs monthly comparison.
Installer App v2 — Jan 2026▼
What shipped: Photo-based installation verification and GPS tagging.
Expected: -15% revisit rate
Actual: -22% revisit rate (exceeded target)
Learnings: GPS tagging helped identify repeat visit clusters — 3 areas in Mumbai accounted for 40% of revisits due to building access issues.
Follow-up: Add pre-installation access check in scheduling flow.
Onboarding Flow v1 — Dec 2025▼
What shipped: Guided onboarding with plan recommendation quiz.
Expected: +10% activation rate
Actual: +6% activation rate (below target)
Learnings: Quiz was too long (8 questions). Users dropped off at question 5. Shortened to 4 questions in hotfix — activation improved to +9%.
Follow-up: Onboarding Wizard v2 planned for Mar 2026.
Speed Test Widget — Oct 2025▼
What shipped: In-app speed test with historical graph.
Expected: Reduce speed-related support tickets by 20%
Actual: Reduced by 28% (exceeded target)
Learnings: Users who ran speed test before calling support had 40% lower call duration. Self-service resolution improved.
Follow-up: Add network health recommendations based on speed test results.
